Turret Defence

Turret Defence expands on my tower-defence experiments with a heavier focus on moment-to-moment gameplay. Modular turrets, wave scripting tools, and responsive UI feedback let me balance quickly without being blocked by tooling.

Unity Rapid Prototyping

Highlights

  • Modular turret architecture that supports quick stat swaps and firing modes.
  • Runtime tuning panel to profile DPS, pathing, and spend efficiency.
  • Stylized FX pass to reinforce damage types and targeting priorities.
