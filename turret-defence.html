Mission Brief

Turret Defence expands on my tower-defence experiments with a heavier focus on moment-to-moment gameplay.

Modular turrets, wave scripting tools, and responsive UI feedback let me balance quickly without being blocked by tooling. The goal was to create a flexible tower defence framework that allows for rapid iteration of turret types and enemy waves.

Core Pillars

  • Modular Architecture: Quick stat swaps and firing modes.
  • Runtime Tuning: Live profiling of DPS and spend efficiency.
  • Stylized FX: Visual feedback for damage types.
