What is it?

Dashing Simulator is a Roblox simulator game designed fully in Roblox Studio, presenting players with a low-poly world, simple enemies, and a relaxing experience with a fun gameplay mechanic to master.

The goal is to climb the leaderboards, either playing solo or joining others to take down enemies. You start in the first 'Area', dashing into enemies to initiate combat. As they chase you, you continue dashing to defeat them and earn Cash rewards. This currency fuels your progression through new areas and upgrades.

Mechanics

  • Dash Combat - Dash into enemies to deal damage.
  • Progression - Unlock areas and Rebirth for multipliers.
  • Collection - Equip pets for stat boosts.
  • Economy - Manage Cash and Gems for upgrades.
