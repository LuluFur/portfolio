Mission Brief

Dashing Simulator is a high-energy Roblox experience focused on fluid movement and speed.

The core loop revolves around mastering the dash mechanic to traverse complex environments and compete with other players. I focused on creating a distinct low-poly aesthetic designed to be readable at high speeds, ensuring players can always read the path ahead.

Core Pillars

  • Fluid Movement: Responsive dash mechanics.
  • Visual Clarity: Low-poly art for high-speed readability.
  • Competition: Leaderboards and race mechanics.
