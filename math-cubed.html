Math Cubed

Arcade-Style Educational Gameplay

Engine Unreal Engine 5
Language Blueprints / C++
Focus Educational Game Design
EdTech Arcade Mechanics UI Design

The Concept

Math Cubed breaks maths drills into satisfying arcade loops. I iterated on reward pacing, tactile UI, and Unreal Blueprint tools so number puzzles feel as punchy as action games. The goal was to remove the "chore" feeling from learning and replace it with the dopamine hit of a high score.
