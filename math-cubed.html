Mission Brief

Math Cubed breaks maths drills into satisfying arcade loops.

I iterated on reward pacing, tactile UI, and Unreal Blueprint tools so number puzzles feel as punchy as action games. The goal was to remove the "chore" feeling from learning and replace it with the dopamine hit of a high score.

Core Pillars

  • Gamification: Arcade loops for education.
  • Tactile UI: Satisfying feedback for correct answers.
  • Difficulty Scaling: Modular problem generation.
