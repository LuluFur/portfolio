Mission Brief

Isosceles Invasion is an arcade defence concept where triangle classifications drive enemy behaviors.

Each wave enforces a new geometry rule, pushing players to reconfigure turret loadouts on the fly. My goal was to gamify geometric concepts into an engaging arcade defence experience, making math concepts intuitive through gameplay.

Core Pillars

  • Geometric Logic: Enemy behavior based on triangle types.
  • Adaptive Strategy: Loadout reconfiguration per wave.
  • Arcade Feel: Juicy hit reactions and scoring.
