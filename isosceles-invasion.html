Isosceles Invasion

Isosceles Invasion is an arcade defence concept where triangle classifications drive enemy behaviors. Each wave enforces a new geometry rule, pushing players to reconfigure turret loadouts on the fly.

Unreal Engine Blueprints Level Design

Highlights

  • Enemy archetypes defined through triangle classifications and adaptive movement rules.
  • Blueprint-driven wave editor for tuning spawn cadence, angle distributions, and scoring.
  • Juicy hit reactions and screen-space UI cues reinforce geometric learning moments.
