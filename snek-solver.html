Mission Brief

Snek Solver is a Unity puzzle project about routing energy paths without painting yourself into a corner.

The prototype let me explore tilemap tooling, juicy grid feedback, and onboarding difficulty ramps. My goal was to build a puzzle game that teaches mechanics through play rather than tutorials, using visual cues to guide the player.

Core Pillars

  • Grid Logic: Pathfinding and spatial reasoning.
  • Juicy Feedback: Responsive tile interactions.
  • Level Design: Progressive difficulty curve.
